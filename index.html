Before Your Night Begins

Things Brides
Often Ask

✦

01

Do you sell or only rent?

Both. Most jewelry sets, caftans, and takchitas are available for sale or rental. Decor items and ammariyas are rental only. Reach out to check availability for your date.

02

Do you ship outside of Chicago?

Yes, we ship across the United States. In-person consultations are available in the Chicago area. Many brides book us entirely remotely.

03

How early should I book?

We recommend 4 to 6 weeks minimum. For full Laylat Lhenna packages, 6 to 8 weeks is ideal. Every detail deserves the time to be perfect.

04

Can I do a full bridal package?

Absolutely. We offer complete Moroccan bridal packages: kaftan, jewelry, mdama belt, Laylat Lhenna setup, and decor, all coordinated for your most sacred day.

05

How do I get a quote?

Message us on WhatsApp or Instagram with your event date, the pieces you love, and your city. We respond within 24 hours, usually sooner.
